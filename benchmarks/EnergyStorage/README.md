# EnergyStorage

Benchmark tasks for electrochemical energy systems, battery charging control, and related optimization problems with safety and lifetime constraints.

See also:

- `PHYSICS.md`: shared physical background, reduced-order modeling notes, and scoring formulas for all EnergyStorage tasks.

## Tasks

- `BatteryFastChargingProfile`: fast-charge current-profile optimization for a lithium-ion cell under voltage, thermal, and degradation constraints.
- `BatteryFastChargingSPMe`: staged fast-charge optimization under a reduced SPMe-T-Aging style electrochemical, thermal, plating, and aging model.
